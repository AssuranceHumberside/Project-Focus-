Why do we need to do this?

As a County Team we have to answer the following question and demonstrate our assurance to HQ.

"Can you be sure that all Sections, Groups, Districts, Campsites and Activities that are run within the County are safe, have adequate written risk assessments and sufficient safety planning...?"

Naturally, we need help to answer this in a positive way and we're going to need everyone's support to achieve this. That's where Project FOCUS was born.
